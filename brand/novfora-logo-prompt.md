# NovFora logo — design prompt (for Gemini / GPT / any image or vector tool)

Copy everything below the line into the model. For image models (Gemini image, GPT‑image), it produces concept renders; for code‑capable models, ask it to "output clean SVG."

---

Design a **logo mark** for **NovFora**, an open‑source, self‑hosted forum and community platform.

**Name meaning:** NovFora = Latin *novus* + *fora* — "new gathering places / new forums."

**Brand world — "Indie Web Hearth + Nova":** warm, cozy, editorial, and independent, with a celestial touch. It's owner‑first software for people who want to *own their community* rather than rent space on a big platform. Tagline: "Own your forum. Build your home." The mood blends a glowing cabin‑on‑a‑hill under a starry sky (hearth, home, warmth) with a *nova* — a new star (fresh, hopeful, modern).

**Core concept to explore:** a **chat / speech bubble holding a four‑point nova spark** — symbolizing *a new conversation, a new forum*. Treat the spark as the hero detail.

**Variations to generate (4–8):**
- Bubble shape: rounded‑rectangle vs circle vs soft‑square app tile.
- Spark placement: centered inside the bubble; breaking through the top‑right edge; or as the three "typing dots" turned into a little constellation.
- Treatment: outline bubble with a solid spark; OR a solid bubble with the spark knocked out as negative space (so it adapts to any background).
- Color: warm amber spark on a cream bubble; and a duotone version pairing amber with a cool "nova blue."

**Style requirements:**
- Modern, minimal, geometric, **flat vector**. No 3D, no heavy gradients (a faint glow is okay), no photographic elements, no drop shadows.
- Must remain legible as a **16px favicon** and in **single‑color / monochrome**.
- Confident and premium, with a slightly editorial/analog warmth. Not corporate‑generic, not childish, not "AI‑sticker."
- It will pair with a **serif wordmark** "NovFora" (an editorial serif such as Newsreader, Spectral, or Source Serif). Keep the mark balanced next to a serif.

**Exact palette (use these hex values):**
- Ember Amber `#EBA94B` — the warm signature / hearth.
- Nova Blue `#4D93F2` — the cool spark / celestial.
- Hearth Cream `#F3E8DD` — light foreground.
- Obsidian `#0B0B10` — dark background.

**Presentation:** show each variation on a dark background (`#0B0B10`) and a light background (`#F3E8DD`), at large size and at favicon size, next to the "NovFora" serif wordmark.

If you can output vector code, return each mark as clean, optimized **SVG** on a 48×48 viewBox with no extraneous metadata.

---

*Context for whoever's iterating: the bubble + nova "new forum" idea is the chosen direction. Other directions already considered and set aside: a compass rose, a plain "N" monogram, a house/speech‑bubble, an orbit‑of‑members ring, and a sunrise‑over‑horizon. Lean into the bubble + spark.*
